About Us

Nepal's First High Tech Indoor Agro Farm

Himalayan Fresh's indoor farm is equipped with an automated system that controls various tasks such as climate control, nutrient balancing, irrigation control, and more. This automation eliminates human dependency and reduces manual errors, leading to higher productivity and improved return on investment.

We are dedicated to providing sustainable and innovative solutions in agriculture, supporting small farmers, and contributing to the economic development of Nepal's horticulture sector.

Greenhouse Complex

The greenhouse complex should have the dual purpose of demonstration (production) /testing/training aimed at growers and of research/education for the students.

E and E Sustainability

Technologies and growing methods that will be considered will make a decisive contribution to increased environmental and economic sustainability and to climate smart horticultural production.

In order to achieve the desired impact, the technologies proposed/ demonstrated/ researched in the Centre should entail limited, stepwise Changes to the existing greenhouse technology.

Improve GDP

To attain a significant improvement in agricultural Gross Domestic Production, exports and private investment & to increase farm income to fight rural poverty.

Silverline Agro Pvt. Ltd.

Silverline Agro Pvt. Ltd. is a company that is focused on building a complete set of agricultural ecology from the nursery to the end user. We are using innovative components developed under a Public-Private Partnership model to provide direct services to small farmers. We are also building a sustainable indoor farm, which utilizes climate-resilient farming techniques. This indoor farm allows plants to be grown under artificial grow lights, independent of the weather outside, resulting in increased growing seasons and higher productivity.
